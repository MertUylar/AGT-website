AGT RACING TEAM

AGT Racing Team is the electric Formula racing team of Atilim University.

Atılım University participated in 2015 for the first time and won the third place in the world in 2016. In this era of rapid development of electric cars, this racing event brings together talented engineers from all over the world and brings a unique experience and experience.

AGT Racing Team manufactures electric golf cars for Atılım University. But the main goal of this team is to bring world degrees to our country and school in SAE organizations. To achieve this, our team continues with students from many engineering branches. AGT Racing Team team will go to restructuring in 2019 in this direction and work with the best engineering students in their field. Another goal of AGT Racing Team is to produce more electric golf vehicles.
